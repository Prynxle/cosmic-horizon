Name Origin

Meet Pluto, the cosmic underdog! This little world got its name from an 11-year-old schoolgirl named Venetia Burney, who was totally into Greek and Roman myths. She suggested naming it after the Roman god of the underworld, and in 1930, the astronomy world said, "That's pretty cool, let's go with it!" Talk about a kid making history! üåë
