Earth

Earth's Characteristics

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only known astronomical object to harbor life. It's a rocky planet with a diverse surface, including land, water, and a breathable atmosphere.

Earth's Significance

With a diameter of about 12,742 kilometers, Earth is the largest of the terrestrial planets. It's home to millions of species, including humans, and has a unique combination of liquid water and plate tectonics that make it habitable.
